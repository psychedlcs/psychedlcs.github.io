Res Week1-3 Notes

发表于 2020-09-21 分类于 课堂笔记

Research method 前3周的Review笔记，重看思路会清晰一些。

Week1

intuition-based hypothesis: 直觉基准理论 e.g. 相似的人互相吸引；互补理论；

derived from: conventional belief, personal experience, authority

measure variables

Byrne & Nelson (1965)

IV: similarity of attitudes

DV: perceived attractiveness

confounders: 同时影响IV和DV的因素 (mediator)

![image-20200920211217151](D:\Blog Files\images2\image-20200920211217151.png)

concept: （概念）
  • the abstract representation of a phenomenon
  • the smallest element in a theory
construct: （观念，由概念构成）

construct of love: 3 sub concepts: intimacy & commitment & passion

relationship: （关系）

structural connection between concept 概念之间的结构关系

包括：

  1. the nature (correlational, casual)
  2. the form (linear, otherwise)
  3. direction (positive, negative)
  4. strength (strong, modest)
theory: （理论）
  • specify relationships among concepts
  • explain the causes
  • predict the consequences
variable: （变量）

vary; 抽象的concept的具体化; 不等于concept

theory是concept的关系 hypothesis是variable的关系

hypothesis

propose: specific, significant, testable & falsifiable, operational/measurable

wording:

  • ○○○ will have higher/lower than ○○○
  • There will be a positive/negative relationship between ○○○ and ○○○
  • As ○○○ increases/decreases, ○○○ will increase/decrease
Alternative H: Ha

the statement we derive to describe a phenomenon

Null H: H0

The statement that is opposite to Ha（Ha的反命题）

有且仅有一个正确

不够肯定的时候可以用

Research Questions

3 types of research questions:
  1. descriptions （了解一个情况）e.g. how frequently…?
  2. explanations （理解关系）e.g. are … related to ?
  3. explorations （探索未知事物）e.g. what do people like and dislike about …?

hypotheses和research questions的区别：

  1. 是statement不是question
  2. 明确relationship between variables
  3. logical and consistent with existing theory or literature

Theory

![image-20200920221440049](D:\Blog Files\images2\image-20200920221440049.png)

how to come up with theory: inductive & deductive reasoning

deductive: 演绎法

从广泛到具体；先预测再观察；从上到下

inductive: 归纳法

从具体到广泛；先观察再预测；从下到上

![image-20200920222519676](D:\Blog Files\images2\image-20200920222519676.png)

G→T→H→O （左下角是inductive，右上角是deductive)

correlation: 相关 （同时发生）

causation: 因果 （先后发生）

confounder也是同时发生

Week2

Qualitative Research Method

use: 未探索的内容、新产品（无法直接得出）

认为researchers’ control may distort the inquiry

seek to gather in-depth, holistic (整体主义的) understanding of human experience in natural settings

![image-20200921083112037](D:\Blog Files\images2\image-20200921083112037.png)

3 types:

  • field observation (去当地现场调查)

    应用：ethnography（人种论）关注客体

    好处：自然环境下获得丰富资料

    坏处：weak generalizability；researcher主观；太贵了

  • focus group（人群研究）

    gather preliminary info., by questionnaire

    应用：marketing and other applied settings

    好处：获得；比采访个人容易

    坏处：weak generalizability；moderator主观

  • in-depth interview

    a single respondent uncover things

    应用：动机、思想等抽象想法，适合敏感话题

    好处：详细观察、获得非口头或书面反馈

    坏处：weak generalizability；researcher主观；不好分析data

Quantitative Methods

3 types:

  • survey

    statistical analysis of responses to questions

    应用：censuses（人口普查）opinion poll（民意调查）

    好处：有理有据、信息（variables）多

    坏处：难定因果关系；自评的偏差（记忆错误、socially desirable answers)

  • experiment

    researcher (1) takes action, (2) observers the consequences in order to examine cause and effect relationships between IV & DV（因果关系）

    应用：心理学、media effect等, best at isolating short-term effects

    好处：可定因果关系；解释大于描述

    坏处：变量少；人工环境（知道自己在被实验）；limited external generalizability

  • content analysis

    systematic analysis of media texts, programs, etc.

    (语料库分析) by decoding

    应用：communication content, real-world

    好处：系统性；可靠

    坏处：不能学习media effects；coding scheme决定；费时间

Week3

Population:

the entire set of people (总体)

Census:

to study every member of the population (普查)

sample:

a subset of the population that is chosen to represent the population (样本)

sampling:

the process by which samples are selected from the study population (抽样)

Goals:

  • Representativeness: similar to the population in all aspects relevant to the research
  • Generalization: can apply to a larger group of obtained data\

只需要看关键属性是否representative

  • sampling unit 抽样单元: smallest element in the population (e.g. 对家庭的研究中，一个家庭就是Unit)
  • sample size 样本量: the number of cases in the complete sample
  • sampling frame 样本框: a complete list of all units of the population

2 types:

  • probability sampling (随机抽样)
  • non-probability sampling (非随机抽样)

non-probability sampling

  1. convenience sampling (便利抽样)

    适用于其他方法都不能用的时候；方便快捷；不够有代表性；

  2. quota sampling (配额抽样)

    sample collected with characteristics of a given cell, and each group is assigned a weight by their portion

    事先按某属性分组，按unit数比例分配

    条件：必须知道population总数；不好分组；需要用力控制变量

  3. snowball sampling (滚雪球抽样)

    一个分发给其他人

    条件：人群不大；互相熟悉；适用于不知道whole population时；但人多需要的工作大，联系不到孤立的样本

  4. volunteer sampling (自愿者抽样)

    只用volunteer

    仅在很多population volunteer的时候；不需要分组；最大的问题是volunteer是谁，比如早来晚来会有属性差异

Probability Sampling Methods

  1. Simple Random Sampling (简单随机抽样)

    listed in fashion (e.g. 数字) 然后随机抽取

  2. Systematic/Sequential Random Sampling (系统随机抽样)

    随意选一个起点，按顺序选取

    1和2条件：必须知道population总数；比随机抽样的样本数少；不一定有representativeness，实际上样本越大越有代表性

  3. Stratified Random Sampling (分层抽样)

    先分组再随机抽样

    条件：必须知道population总数；更有代表性、好控制；分组涉及主观选择

  4. Cluster Sampling (群集抽样)

    clusters: city blocks, classes at a university, etc.

    群体中的每个成员都是example

    可能会涉及到多阶段抽样（如：某楼→某层→某间）

    条件：适用于不知道whole population，但知道组别时；效率高；不如随机和分层抽样准确

  5. Random Digit Dialing (RDD)

    根据某区域内电话号随机选择

    条件：适用于不知道whole population；效率高；可能会根据phone占有率有偏差
