Week4

concept → operationalization

e.g. social networking site usage → hours spent on FB, Wechat, etc.

Three steps of operationalization
  1. Deciding unit of analysis
  2. conceptual definition
  3. operational definition

4 ways of quantification(量化)

Nominal Measures (定类)

no mathematical interpretation e.g. male1, female2

Ordinal measures (定序)

only order e.g. size of dog: small, medium, large

Interval Measures(定距)

no 0 point e.g. IQ, Fahrenheit Temperature

Ratio Measures(定比)

with an absolute 0 point (absolutely no amount of the quality)

Likert Scale: treated as interval
triangulation（三角测量）

using multiple methods / operationalization to study the same phenomenon

Evaluation of Measurements

Reliability (信度):

measurement consistency (how much error or noise exists in your measurement) 检验的结果是否可靠

  1. test-retest reliability: 做2次相同的测试，检验结果的相关系数

    缺点：通常很难做2次，答案随时间变化

  2. split-half reliability: 被测分成2组，检验结果的相关系数

  3. multiple item scale: 用多个相同的问题进行测试，检验结果的相关系数

Validity (效度)

whether is the measurement actually measures the construct rather than something else 是否检验了要检验的问题

not easily quantified

Subjective Validity:
  1. Face Validity (表面效度) 受测者的主观感受
  2. Content Validity (内容效度) evaluated by experts in the content area
Criterion-based Validity:
  1. Concurrent Validity (共时效度) : Correspondence of one measure of a concept with another of the same or similar concept 使用以前的已有测试，同时进行相同的研究
  2. Predictive Validity (预测效度)：The measure is predictive of other things that it is supposedly able to predict

Week5

Survey Research

Research based on asking people questions and the analysis of responses to those questions

Advantages Disadvantages
realistic setting hard to detect causality
reasonable cost accuracy for self-reports
large amount of information from a variety of people opinion on unfamiliar issues
existing supportive source fear of telemarketing
Cross-sectional surveys (横切):

surveys that study respondents at one point in time.

Longitudinal surveys (纵切):

surveys that study respondents at several points in time. 3 types:

  1. a trend study (趋势研究): beliefs, attitudes, behaviors
  2. a cohort study (世代研究): gather responses from a specific population who share a common characteristic or experience at different periods
  3. a panel study (定群研究): obtains responses from the same group of participants over time

![image-20201101223314570](D:\Blog Files\images2\image-20201101223314570.png)

  1. Likert-Type Scales (李克特量表)

  2. Semantic Differential Scales (语义差异量表)

    Pleasant _ _ _ _ _ _ _ Unplesant

  3. Thurstone Scales: assign values from 1 to 11 to each item

  4. feeling thermometer scale (温度计量表)

  5. Smiling face scale

Guidelines for Design

Questions:
  • Keep language simple (no jargon, no W-negative)
  • Express only a single idea
  • Define your terms
  • Avoid leading questions
Response options:
  • The middle option
  • Numerical labeling of responses
  • Open vs. closed ended responses (better result: pilot study to a small group of people for open-ended results, then move to close-ended responses)
  • Response option order: primacy effect (to select earlier responses) ;recency effect (to select later responses) salient in telephone surveys
  • Social desirable responses: 可以通过保密、措辞、制造不好的回答也很常见的印象等方式避免
Order of questions:
  • From simple to complicated questions
  • From general to specific questions
  • From easy to difficult questions
  • From insensitive to sensitive questions
  • Avoid potential impacts of previous questions on
    subsequent questions (e.g., hints, framing,
    expectations)

statistics (量数): an estimated value based on a sample

a descriptive statistic (描述性统计): only a quantitative characteristic of the sample

an inferential statistic (推论统计): by which a sample statistic can correctly estimate a popular parameter

Descriptive statistics

measures of central tendency

Interval Scale (定量):

  • Mode: 众数
  • Median: 中位数
  • Mean: 平均数

Nominal Scale (定性):

Percentage (pi =fi / n, 60/100 female, then p = 0.6)

→ for spread or variability of the data values:

measures of dispersion
  • range: max - min
  • variance: s^2, σ^2, (i.e.∑(x-x)^2/(n-1))
  • standard deviation: s (i.e. SRQT∑(x-x)^2/(n-1)* )

Nominal Scale:

– Variance (s2): p(1-p)/n-1

– Standard Deviation (s): SQRT[p(1-p)/n-1]

Hypothesis Testing (假设检验)

Confidence Interval (置信区间):

gives an estimated range of sample means which is likely to include an unknown population mean.

P-Value & significance level (显著水平):

Communicates the chance of being wrong where claiming a difference/effect.

an opinion poll should always report its margin of error (normally ±4% given a 95% C.I.)
